=========
Shortcuts
=========

Here is the list of shortcuts you can use during a presentation:

- ``<DownArrow>``, ``<RightArrow>``, ``<Space>``, ``<PageDown>`` to go to the next step of the presentation,
- ``<UpArrow>``, ``<LeftArrow>``, ``PageUp`` to go to the previous step of the presentation,
- ``s`` to open the speaker notes,
- ``i/j/k/l`` to move the scrolling window,
- ``z/Shift + Z`` to zoom/unzoom,
- ``t`` to open the table of content.

In addition, the drawing shortcuts are more discoverable:

- ``p`` to select the "Pen" tool,
- ``h`` to select the "Highlighter" tool,
- ``e`` to select the "Eraser" tool,
- ``x`` to select the "Cursor" tool,
- ``Shift + X`` to clear the drawing canvas.
- ``Shift + R`` to open the recording manager.

Finally, when the recording manager is open you have the following shortcut:

- ``Shift + R`` to start recording
- ``s`` to select the "Select" tool,
- ``m`` to select the "Move" tool,
- ``r`` to select the "Resize" tool,
